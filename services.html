Our Services

Every Friday | West End AFC, Denton

Friday Small Group Football Sessions

Our Friday sessions are delivered in small groups of up to 15 players for 60 minutes, providing young footballers aged 6+ with extra contact time and detailed coaching in a focused learning environment.

Sessions are structured to improve individual possession, passing and receiving, combining with teammates, attacking and defending principles, and shooting actions, with training progressing from ball mastery into opposed practices and competitive small-sided games.

Players experience game-realistic scenarios such as 1v1s, 2v1s, 2v2s and small-sided matches, allowing learning to transfer directly into 7v7, 9v9 and 11v11 game environments. Sessions are adaptable to individual learning objectives set by clubs or personal development needs.

Cost: £10 per session or £35 for the full monthly block.

School Holidays | West End AFC (Indoor Facility)

Holiday Football & Multi-Sport Camps

Route One Holiday Camps are delivered in partnership with West End AFC at their indoor astro facility, ensuring sessions run regardless of weather. Camps are open to children aged 6+ of all abilities.

Camps prioritise football development through individual ball work, 1v1s, 2v2s, passing and shooting practices, skill games, races and mini matches, alongside a variety of fun multi-sport and P.E-style activities.

Full Day (9am–3pm): £25 per child
Half Day (9am–12pm): £15 per child
Sibling and West End AFC player discounts available.
