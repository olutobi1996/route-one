Our Services

Every Friday | Manchester

Friday Football Camps

Our Friday Camps are designed for children of all ages and abilities. Held in Manchester, these sessions focus on developing fundamental football skills while keeping the experience fun and engaging. Children enjoy drills, small-sided games, and skill challenges that improve their confidence, teamwork, and love for the game.

Each session is carefully planned to ensure a safe, positive environment where young players can learn, grow, and have fun every week. Perfect for kids looking to stay active after school and develop their football abilities.

During School Terms | Manchester

Term-Time Multi-Sport Camps

Our Term-Time Camps provide young players with a mix of football and multi-sport sessions that develop skills, coordination, and teamwork. Designed for children of all ages, these camps run during school terms and offer structured activities that are both educational and fun.

Sessions include technical drills, fitness games, and mini-tournaments that build confidence and a love for sport. With a focus on long-term development rather than just short-term results, children improve their abilities in a supportive, encouraging environment.
