About Route One

Route One Junior Performance Development is built around creating confident, technically strong, and motivated young players in a positive and professional environment.

About Us

Route One Junior Performance Development is a growing football coaching programme dedicated to supporting young players through high-quality, engaging, and structured training sessions.

We provide a positive environment where children can build confidence, develop technical ability, and enjoy learning the game. Our sessions are carefully planned to challenge players while keeping football fun and accessible for all abilities.

Alongside our regular training, we deliver popular holiday camps and weekly Friday sessions, giving players consistent opportunities to develop, stay active, and grow both on and off the pitch.

Our Aim

Our aim at Route One is to engage young players from an early age, creating strong foundations for a healthy, active lifestyle through football and sport.

We are committed to helping children stay active, build confidence, and develop a positive relationship with exercise. Our sessions are designed to encourage movement, learning, and enjoyment in every environment we deliver.

By focusing on long-term development rather than short-term results, we aim to inspire a lifelong love for sport while supporting each player’s individual journey.

Route One Football Coaching

Fun, inclusive and professional football coaching for children of all abilities. Based in the UK, delivering high-quality sessions and holiday camps.
