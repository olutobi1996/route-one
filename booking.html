Book Your Sessions

All Route One sessions are delivered in small groups of up to 15 players for 60 minutes, allowing detailed coaching focused on individual development in a professional and supportive environment.

Sessions focus on improving individual possession, passing and receiving, combining with teammates in and out of possession, attacking and defending principles, and shooting actions. Training progresses from unopposed ball mastery into opposed practices and competitive small-sided game scenarios.

NEW: Individual Development Session – Mondays 8–9pm at West End AFC. Designed for experienced Boys & Girls aged 13+ already performing at a high grassroots or academy level. Advanced, high-intensity sessions focus on positional detail, technical repetition, physical development, opposed practices and real match scenarios. Delivered in structured 4-week blocks with regular contact time and performance monitoring across games and training. Train like an academy player. Meet Elite.

Prices are £10 per session or £35 when booked for the full month. Occasional Friday sessions may be moved in line with Rochdale AFC fixtures.

To book, select your preferred session via the calendar or contact directly via WhatsApp on 07969 433767 or email RouteOneJunior_PD@hotmail.com.
